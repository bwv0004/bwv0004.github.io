Sod Varieties Available

419 Bermuda

Details
Ideal golf course fairways and tees. It is also used on football, soccer and baseball fields due to its ability to recover quickly from ware and stand up to heat and sun. Use 419 Bermuda for any sod and turfgrass needs for high use or highly visible areas in warm climates.

Z52 Zoysia

Details
Flexible warm season turfgrass utilized for many applications. Meyers Z-52 has become the most popular of the Zoysia grasses because of its uniform medium texture and tolerances for heat and cold. It provides a plush, high density, deep green cover mid-April through early October in the Southwest. Recommended for residential and commercial landscapes, golf courses, athletic fields and recreation areas.

centipede

Details
Well adapted to the sandy, acidic soils of low fertility and requires low maintenance. It spreads by stolons and has a coarse texture with short upright stems that grow to about 3 to 5 inches and requiring less mowing, survives in mild cold temperatures as long as there aren not several hard freezes since it does not go into a true dormancy and with light freezes will turn brown but as soon as the temperature rises it will recover and re-greens.
