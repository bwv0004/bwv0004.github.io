FAQs


How much area does 1 pallet cover?
Each pallet covers 450 sqft (50 yards). There are 170 16"x24" pieces on a pallet.
What grass works best in shady areas?
Zoysia and centipede are shade tolerant.

How far in advance do I need to place my order?
Please contact us 24-48 hours ahead of time. Since all sod is cut fresh to order, this will allow us appropriate time to dispatch our harvestor and loading/delivery equipment. Weather conditions could affect the timing for your order.
