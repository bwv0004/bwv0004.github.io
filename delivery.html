Delivery Options


Professional Delivery

If you place an order for at least 6 pallets of sod, we will deliver the sod to your job site and place the pallets where you want them.

Pickup at Our Farm

Orders less than 6 pallets will need to be picked up at our farm. We will load the sod onto your vehicle/trailer. Keep in mind that depending on moisture if the sod, a pallet can weigh between 1500-3000 lbs
